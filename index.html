Sustainability

At Mirthy Earth, our aim is to procure sustainable, biodegradable products without compromising on style or luxury. Our range of wheat straw products allows you to enjoy life in good conscience. Wheat straw plastics are biodegradable, contains no gluten, and is tough and strong. Our products are microwave and dishwasher friendly.
